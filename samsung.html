manufactured by Samsung and noted for its combination
of a large display, 5.55-inch (141 mm) measured diagonally, 
and software optimized for an integral self-storing stylus. 
Because the display places the device at the large end of 
the smartphone and small end of the tablet size ranges, 
the Galaxy Note II has been called a hybrid of a phone and tablet, or phablet.
